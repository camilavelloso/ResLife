Code Red: Baltimore's Climate Divide


About the project: "Code Red" explored the impact of climate change on public health in Baltimore and recently won the National Press Foundation’s Innovative Storytelling Award. The project was a collaboration among the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism, Capital News Service and NPR.

Design process: Defined requirements for the website, created a schedule and brainstormed ideas; compiled a list of examples from several news organizations that would serve as inspiration for the website’s typography, story layout, splash page, graphics and other elements; produced a mock-up of the landing page and secondary story page on Adobe Photoshop; created a prototype of my website - based on the Photoshop mock-ups - using Adobe XD; coded my design in Atom using HTML, CSS, jQuery and JavaScript.


Growing Up Behind Bars


About the project: "Behind Bars" is an investigative project that examines the state of juvenile justice in the United States, the only country to condemn people to life in prison without parole for crimes committed as minors. The project was a collaboration among the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism, Capital News Service and PBS NewsHour.

Design process: Defined website requirements, developed a schedule and researched example typography, layout and colors,; coded my design in Atom using HTML, CSS, jQuery and JavaScript.
